Layer 0 Context: The Agentic Reasoning Protocol was born from a simple observation: AI systems hallucinate about brands millions of times daily, and domain owners have no mechanism to correct this at the retrieval layer. Sascha Deforth, working from Düsseldorf, Germany, designed reasoning.json as the cognitive complement to schema.org. Where schema.org teaches machines WHAT things are, reasoning.json teaches machines HOW to think about them. The protocol was released as MIT-licensed open source in March 2026 through Hope and Glory Studio, with TrueSource (truesource.studio) serving as the reference implementation and professional auditing platform. reasoning.json is the first web standard designed not for crawlers or indexers, but for reasoning agents.
